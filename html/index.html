About Me

Raksa is a third-year Bachelor of Science student in Software Development at the American University of Phnom Penh and a second-year Bachelor of Arts student in English at the Royal University of Phnom Penh. A recipient of multiple competitive scholarships, including full scholarships from AMT, the Ministry of Education, Youhts and Sports, Acleda University of Business, and Spring Education Center. He graduated high school in 2023 with top academic results and an “A” on the Cambodian National Examination (Bac II). Beyond academics, Raksa has co-led and contributed to national-level education and technology initiatives. He has served as a Student Ambassador Leader, community fellow, and housing representative, organizing large-scale academic events, industry meetups, university outreach programs, and multi-session webinars in collaboration with government bodies and industry experts. His work reflects a strong commitment to leadership, structured execution, and advancing digital education in Cambodia.
